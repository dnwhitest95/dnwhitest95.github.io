Projects

Title: Studies Toward the Synthesis Of A Novel Plant Hormone Imaging Agent

1-Aminocyclopropane-1-carboxylic Acid (ACC) is a progenitor to the gaseous plant hormone ethylene. We are currently using classic organic chemistry methodologies from readily available materials to synthesize 2‐halo‐ACC analogues. These analogues will be investigated for phenotypic responses in the plant model Arabidopsis thaliana. In theory the response from ACC should be similar to that of ethylene. The long‐term goal of this work is to develop a practical route for the radiochemical synthesis of [18F]‐ACC as a novel plant hormonal positron emission topography [PET] imaging agent.
